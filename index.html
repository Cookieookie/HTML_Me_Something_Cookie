BONUS: Skylight Cave

Ash Grove, MO | Roundtrip Length: Unknown | Tip: Bring a headlamp and some waterproof shoes

Hidden in the small town of Ash Grove, MO, skylight cave is a beautiful local gem that features a special room called "The Autograph Room" which was created by an underground whirlpool and houses a number of wall carvings dating as far back as the late 1800's. In order to access this cave, you must get permission from the landowner that it resides on or enter through a back neighborhood at your own risk.
